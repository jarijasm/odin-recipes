Red Velvet Cake Recipe

The most delicious red velvet cake you will ever try. It is moist, delicious, and just melts in your mouth. With a hint of chocolate flavor and it's signature bright red color, this will become one your most favorite recipes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1-2 oz. of red food coloring, the more you add the brighter the end result
  • 1/2 cup brewed hot coffee
  • 1 tsp white vinegar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Butter or spray two 8 inch round cake pans and line with parchment paper.
  3. In a bowl whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, cocoa powder, and salt.
  4. In another bowl combine the sugar and vegetable oil.
  5. Add the eggs, buttermilk, vanilla extract, and red food coloring to the bowl with the sugar and oil and mix until everything is well combined.
  6. Mix in the coffe and white vinegar to the bowl with the wet ingredients and mix until combined.
  7. Slowly add the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until well combined. The batter will be thin.
  8. Pour the batter evenly between the two cake pans.
  9. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  10. Cool in the pans for 30 minutes then remove from the pans carefully and place on a cooling rack until completely cooled.
  11. Frost with your favorite frosting and enjoy!
