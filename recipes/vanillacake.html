Vanilla Cake Recipe

So many different vanilla cakes on the web, but this one is the best tasting one I have found. It is buttery, rich, and full of flavor. Goes well with any king of filling and any type of frosting. You won't be disappointed.

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks of butter, softened to room temperature
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 5 large eggs, room temperature
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup whole milk, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk, room temperature
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Butter or spray three 8 inch round cake pans and line with parchment paper.
  3. Cream together butter and shortening until light and fluffy with an electric mixer.
  4. Slowly add sugar one cup at a time, making sure to incorporate each cup before adding the other.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, making sure to incorporate each egg before adding another.
  6. Sift the flour, baking powder, and salt into a bowl and set aside.
  7. Pour the milk, buttermilk,and vanilla extract into a measuring cup and whisk together.
  8. Add the flour mixture alternating with the milk mixture to the butter and shortening, starting and ending witht the flour.
  9. Gently stir until well combined. Scrape sides with a spatula.
  10. Pour evenly between the cake pans and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  11. When a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, it is ready.
  12. Cool in cake pans for 5 minutes then carefully remove from the pans and place on a cooling rack to cool completely.
  13. Frost and enjoy!
