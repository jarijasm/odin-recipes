Chocolate Cake Recipe

The most delicious and moist chocolate cake you will ever make. Soft, delicate crumb with a rich chocolate taste. One of the easiest recipes you will make.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cups unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup freshly brewed hot coffee

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Butter or spray two 8 inch round cake pans and line with parchment paper.
  3. Sift the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt into a bowl. Mix until combined
  4. In another bowl mix buttermilk, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract until combined.
  5. Slowly add the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix to combine.
  6. Add the hot hot brewed coffee and mix. Scrape the bowl with a rubber spatula to make sure everything is well combined.
  7. Pour batter evenly between the two pans and bake for about 35-40 minutes. Cake is done when toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean.
  8. Cool in the pan for 30 minutes, then take them out and place on a cooling rack to completely cool.
  9. Frost and enjoy!
